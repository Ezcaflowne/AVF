The top selling app categories in the U.S. are game apps.

What are the 'top selling' app categories in Japan?

Some of the best selling app categories in Japan are utilities, like comic book readers, and handwritten note taking apps.

What are the 'top selling' app categories in Great Britain?

The top selling app categories in Great Britain are the paid apps that are dominated by games and the free apps by utilities.
